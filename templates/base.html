About Us

Castle Apartments Ltd. was founded in April-May 2019 by five scholars attending their studies of computer science at Reykjavik University, Iceland. The main focus of the company is in selling high quality old fashion family homes, providing a platform for buyers and sellers to communicate in an easy and sufficient matter through our website service, guaranteeing a satisfying way to buy/sell properties. Our goals are to have our customers leaving satisfied with the service we provide, providing fast solutions to the modern day problems of finding your dream home and/or selling your real estate fast and easy.
